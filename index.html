Virginia offers virtual counselling services to individuals, couples and families of all ages and backgrounds that reside permanently or temporarily in Alberta. In person sessions may be requested and offered occasionally in Calgary, although the majority of the counselling will be virtual. Sessions take place through the Jane platform which is an online platform created for health professionals. Jane is very easy to use and requires no downloads.
Virginia operates under the belief that people are doing the best that they can with the resources that they have, considering their histories and the specific circumstances of their lives. She believes that the great majority of people want to be well and do well for themselves and others. She works to support people to better understand themselves and their situation; to develop self compassion; and to increase their mental, emotional, spiritual and relational resources, so that they can live more fulfilling lives.
